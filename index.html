QUESTIONS


Will there be guest shuttles on the day-of?

We ask that guests be responsible for their own transportation to and from the ceremony.

Without a car, we recommend Uber or Lyft. Your driver can drop you off in front of the chapel at the drop-off circle off of Cleveland Avenue North.

With a car, park on the campus of the University of St. Thomas. See directions below.

Our reception venue is a short walk from the Canopy and Radisson hotels and a short distance by car or train from the Graduate hotel.

Where can I find parking for the ceremony and reception?

At the ceremony, park on the campus of the University of St. Thomas at either Tommie North (N1) or Tommie East (E1) parking garages or surface lots A, B, D, or G (ignore permit requirement signage, campus map: parking circled in red and chapel in blue).

At the reception, we recommend you reserve parking at the Riverfront Municipal Parking Ramp ahead of time to secure a spot given an event that night at U.S. Bank Stadium. Click the button below to book for a flat rate.

We have so many favorite activities, but here are our top suggestions:

the happy couple, pictured post-proposal (2020)

Shan next to her favorite Flour Tower exhibit (2016)

Shan and Dan's summer Twins game tradition (2014)

the Surly beer Shan and Dan found during their stint in Palo Alto (2019)
